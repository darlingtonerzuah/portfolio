Wavemap

Last updated: April 4, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Wavemap ("the app") handles your information. By using the app you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Wavemap scans nearby WiFi networks to provide heatmap visualization, network diagnostics, and WiFi compass navigation. This includes network names (SSIDs), signal strength (RSSI), BSSID identifiers, frequency bands, and security types. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external server except where explicitly stated.

2. Location Permission

WiFi scanning on Android requires location permission as mandated by Google. We use this permission solely to scan WiFi networks. We do not track, store, or share your physical location.

3. Camera Permission

The WiFi Compass feature requires camera access to display the live camera feed as a background. We do not record, store, or transmit any camera data. The camera feed never leaves your device.

4. Sensor Access

The WiFi Compass feature uses your device's compass and gyroscope to track the direction of strongest WiFi signal. Sensor data is processed entirely on-device and is never stored or transmitted.

5. Data Storage

Heatmap data, network preferences, and app settings are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.

6. Third Party Services

Wavemap communicates with a backend server hosted on Render (render.com) for network ping measurements. No personal data is sent to this server. The server only receives anonymous ping requests.

7. Notifications

If enabled, Wavemap sends local notifications when a scanned WiFi signal drops below -70 dBm. These notifications are generated entirely on-device and do not involve any external service.

8. Children's Privacy

Wavemap is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the updated date at the top of this page.
