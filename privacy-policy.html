WiFi AR

Last updated: April 4, 2026

This privacy policy describes how WiFi AR ("the app") handles your information. By using the app you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

WiFi AR scans nearby WiFi networks to provide heatmap visualization and diagnostics. This includes network names (SSIDs), signal strength (RSSI), BSSID identifiers, and frequency bands. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external server except where explicitly stated.

2. Location Permission

WiFi scanning on Android requires location permission as mandated by Google. We use this permission solely to scan WiFi networks. We do not track, store, or share your physical location.

3. Camera Permission

The AR feature requires camera access to display the live camera feed. We do not record, store, or transmit any camera data.

4. Data Storage

Heatmap data and app preferences are stored locally on your device using shared preferences. This data never leaves your device.

5. Third Party Services

The app communicates with a backend server hosted on Render (render.com) for diagnostics ping measurements. No personal data is sent to this server.

6. Children's Privacy

WiFi AR is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the updated date at the top of this page.
